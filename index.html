Spaceship

This is a spaceship I created as an assignment for a web animation course I took.The system I used to create the design and animation for this gif was Blender, an opensource software available for creatives.

When approaching the design for this concept, I wanted to stray away from the typical spaceship saucer look that everyone thinks about.

Also, I wanted to give a sporty look to this spaceship and so I decided to with a rectangularshape and use the color red as a prominent color. This was one of my favorite gifs I created in blender and this assignment sparked my interest in animation.

Word of the Day Initiative

I am a member of the Queens College Japanese Culture Club. In addition, I am one of the designers and social media moderators for the club social media page. I created the "Word of the Week" project because many sudents in the club besides myself are studying Japanese. In addition, many of the students who are not studying the language have expressed their desire to learn.

Therefore, with the "Word of the Week" project, I have provided two solutions. For the students who have not begun learning Japanese, I have included romaji so they will be able to read the word and example sentences. As for the Japanese language studying students, I am including vocabulary that are not included in the textbooks however, they are commonly used in Japan. Therefore, they can increase their vocabulary knowledge in addition t studying the materia from their clases.
