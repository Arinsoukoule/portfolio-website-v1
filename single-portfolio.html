"Fall seven times, stand up eight" or 「七転び八起き」is a Japanese proverb that Daruma represent.

 

Project Name:  Daruma Infographic
Software: Illustrator
Year: 2020

Daruma are a Japanese lucky charm that I personally feel a connection to because of the message they represent which is to never give up. Therefore as the designer and social media coordinator for the Japanese Culture Club at Queens College, I decided to highlight this special charm for an instagram series I created called Culture Time.

Culture Time is one of the concepts I created for the Japanese Culture Club's social media pages. Through this series, the goal has been to engage the members of the club as Japanese culture and language learners in more information and topics related to Japan. I have had fun designing various assets for the Culture Time series and increasing the engagement on the Japanese Culture Club's social media page.
